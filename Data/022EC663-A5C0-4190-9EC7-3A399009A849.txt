This research is a plan of two years. In the first year, the main purpose is to 
improve the performance of passband of a film bulk acoustic wave resonance (FBAR 
filter). This membrane-type FBAR used aluminum nitride as its piezoelectric material. 
We successfully fabricated a membrane- type AlN FBAR by using ICP process, and 
designed a ladder type bandpass filter. We found that by changing the top electrode 
material and structure of each FBAR can improve the passband characteristic of the 
ladder type filter. 
 
In the second year, we developed a duplexer based on our pervious FBAR filter. 
This duplexer has transmitted and received band pass of 60 MHz bandwidth from 
1850 to 1910 MHz and 1930 to 1990 MHz, respectively.
