inconvenient in the daily life because of the 
drawback of the vision function. Along of the 
technological advance, the technique that 
uses zigbee wireless sensor network to carry 
out the humanâ€™s positioning system is 
gradually mature. The computer receives the 
position of the visual impairment to calculate 
the direction and distance of the house 
furnishings, doors, windows, bathrooms, 
sleeping apartments and appliances in the 
living home, through uses the tool of the Text 
To Speech (TTS) to tell the visual 
impairmentâ€™s whole conditions.     
This project mainly designs a speech 
assistant system for visual impairment in the 
living home to help to provide the visual 
impairment the information of the whole 
environments. It can let not only visual 
impairment makes a convenience of the life, 
more can take place the danger send out the 
warning. The coordinate system of the living 
home of the visual impairment is set up at 
first. Several reference nodes of the zigbee 
wireless sensor network are built in the living 
home. The coordinator of the house 
furnishings, doors, windows, bathrooms, 
sleeping apartments and appliances in the 
living home was keyed in the personal 
computer (PC). Then we will develop the 
portable instrument using the zigbee wireless 
sensor network to examine the information
