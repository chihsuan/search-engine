This invention proposes an efficient approach to synthesize unique 
carbon nanostructure. The fabrication process is briefly described as 
follows. At first, the commercial carbon materials were immersed in 
metallic solution. After that, the transition metal or metal oxide 
nanoparticles were deposited onto the carbon surface after heat 
treatment. Then a chemical vapor deposition was employed to grow the 
carbon nanotubes decorated with the carbon matrix, thus forming 
unique carbon nanostructure. The as-synthesized carbon material was 
found to have high specific surface area and porosity, determined form 
physisorption of nitrogen at 77 K.
