Abstract 
Optimization problem is one of bio-
logical research questions. Bioinformatics 
field is developed rapidly in recent years, the 
capacity of the data is increasing constantly, 
biologists collect and store a great quantity of 
experimental data, utilize technology of the 
database. Utilize new technology to speed up 
the investigation, provokes data mining tech-
nique to have very good development oppor-
tunities. In this project, we employ the 
Query-Based Learning Method to apply in 
data mining processes, thereby efficiently 
improving the overall performance. It can 
effectively refine any irrelevant, deviation, 
redundant data include eliminating the inter-
mediary information or rule of without rep-
resentativeness at preprocessing phase. The 
proposed method can reduce the time and 
cost in mining processes, dispel duplicate and 
unimportant information, not only can re-
trench efforts but also improve knowledge 
accuracy and usability.  
Keywords: Bioinformatics, optimization, 
data mining, query-based method

Abstract 
Optimization problem is one of bio-
logical research questions. Bioinformatics 
field is developed rapidly in recent years, the 
capacity of the data is increasing constantly, 
biologists collect and store a great quantity of 
experimental data, utilize technology of the 
database. Utilize new technology to speed up 
the investigation, provokes data mining tech-
nique to have very good development oppor-
tunities. In this project, we employ the 
Query-Based Learning Method to apply in 
data mining processes, thereby efficiently 
improving the overall performance. It can 
effectively refine any irrelevant, deviation, 
redundant data include eliminating the inter-
mediary information or rule of without rep-
resentativeness at preprocessing phase. The 
proposed method can reduce the time and 
cost in mining processes, dispel duplicate and 
unimportant information, not only can re-
trench efforts but also improve knowledge 
accuracy and usability.  
Keywords: Bioinformatics, optimization, 
data mining, query-based method
