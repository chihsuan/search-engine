straw-like tangible interface that use emotion metaphor to convey meaningful interaction and

settings. We take a design probe approach to exploring design alternatives. The design probe 
includes mock-up experiments, functional sketch, and script writing. The current version of the 
iAWN prototype is equipped with innovative reminding technology, which combines the 
Livindex Cloud with the Google Calendar service. Through an openminded ideation process, 
users could provide their feedbacks and suggestions to our gestural interface design in an 
interactive smart space, and enrich the characters of the iAWN far beyond the capabilities of a 
single design team. As of currently, the achievements from this study have actually provided us 
with an adjustable environment to improve our understanding of issues related to 
actionperception coupling and gesture meanings. Future work is to make iAWN devices as a 
seamlessly service platform for awareness of energy consumption, environmental quality, and 
personal health conditions.
