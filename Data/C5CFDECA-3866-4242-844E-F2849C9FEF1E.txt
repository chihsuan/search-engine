 Abstract 
KeywordsWooden Building	Floor	Ambient Vibration Measuring	
Impact Vibration 
 
During the 1999 Chi-Chi, Taiwan earthquake, numerous wooden 
historic buildings suffered serious damage. Most of these buildings are 
important cultural property in Taiwan. The damages of wooden historic 
building were not only due to the large earthquake magnitude, but also 
the lack of consideration for the impact loading during the reuse process 
of two-story wooden building. Considering impact loading during the 
reuse process needs to take into account the dynamic characteristics of the 
wooden floor, including the different amount of tourists, and the different 
behaviors of action are tested by vibration measurement.  
The contents includes 
1. To experiment by ambient vibration measuring to find out the 
fundamental period of the two-story wooden historic buildings. 
 
2. To build data base of the fundamental periods, and to discover the 
characteristic of the wooden historic buildings after strengthening. 
     
3. To provide references to diagnose their relationship with the 
structural safety and the vibration of the wooden floors under 
various loading and impact behaviors. 
 
4. According to different case, the limited amounts of visitant are 
suggested.
