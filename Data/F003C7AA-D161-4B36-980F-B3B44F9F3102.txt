  In this project, second-order fiber Bragg gratings is fabricated and measured.  The phase mask 
with UV light is use to manufacture a second-order fiber Bragg grating.  The transmission and 
the reflection spectrum are measure in this project.  Since the radiation loss is the most 
important property for the second-order Bragg condition, the radiation loss is obtained by 
analyzing the measured spectrum.  Outside the resonant region, the measured radiation loss is 
1.577dB, while in the resonant region, we obtained the radiation loss of 1.017dB.
